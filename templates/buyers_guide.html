Buying Crusader Kings II


Crusader kings II has been out for {% print(year-2012) %}, and in those years many DLCs have come out. Some better than others. If you were to buy the game and all its DLC you would be spending over $300. Thankfully, like many of Paradox Interactive games, Crusader Kings II goes on sale often. The DLC I believe are essential for Crusader Kings II are:

  • The Old Gods
  • The Reapers Due
  • Way of life
  • Sons of Abraham


There are also portrait, unit pack, and music packs DLCs. They are not essential, but if you feeling you need pretty portraits and music I recommend getting the ultimate packs. They are bundles that include all the music, portraits, and unit packs released for Crusader Kings II. You of course have the option of buying separate portrait, unit, and music packs, but the bundle will save you the most money.

  • Collection – Crusader Kings II: Dynasty Shield Pack
  • Collection – Crusader Kings II: Ultimate Music Pack
  • Collection – Crusader Kings II: Ultimate Unit Pack
  • Collection – Crusader Kings II: Ultimate Portrait Pack


Once you have the game, along with any desired DLC, you can visit the Beginners Guide to learn some of the basics of the game. Note: the guide will only show you the basics of the base game (no DLC required).

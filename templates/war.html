Expanding your realm


Warfare

There are a couple of ways of expanding your realm. The more forceful way of expanding is through warfare. I order to declare war you need a Casus Belli, a reason for war.

  • Pressing claims: You can press your own on other lands if the land in question is part of your duchy, kingdom, or empire. If you do not have claims to press, you can use your Chancellor to fabricate a claim on land you want. You also have the option of inviting title claimants to your court and pressing their claims.
  • Religious wars: If you border land near people of a different religious group than you, you can declare a religious war. If you win you will be able to take whole duchies fro your opponent.


Diplomacy

If fighting wars is not your thing, then maybe try dipolmacy! There are a couple of ways to diplomatically expand.

  • Diplomatic vassalization: If you are at least two ranks higher than your target, you are their de jure liege, and they are the same culture as you then that character is likely to peacefully join your nation.
  • Marriage: If you can not claim land in a war, then why not marry up? Through careful planning and a bit of intrigue you can set your future heirs up more land. You can try to marry power families in hopes to inherit their land and power, you can ensure it happens with a couple of well placed assassins.
